Welcome to the docs! You can start writing documents in `/content/docs`.

**Nexus UI** is a component library built on top of shadcn/ui, purpose-designed for AI interfaces. Rather than retrofitting general-purpose components, every primitive is crafted around the patterns AI products demand — streaming states, conversational flows, confidence indicators, and dynamic content rendering. Composable, themeable, and production-ready, Nexus UI gives you the building blocks to ship polished AI experiences without reinventing the foundation.
